Discover Life on Tanti

Resort

Tanti's premier resort is famed for it's golf course. Eighteen holes of rough lava rock, small sand bars, inland salt water pools, and a breathtaking fairway.

Hotels

From theme Hotels to inexpensive chains and family owned Hotels, Tanti has lodging covered for everyone.

Hostels

Tanti is for everyone. With that in mind our Island is home to a variety of comfortable and save youth Hostels.

Beach Sports

Beach Volleyball and Surfing are some of the ever enjoyable activities on Tanti's Beaches. Waves are best on the West side Beaches near the Reef.

Relaxing Beaches

Family Beaches are available for relaxation, sand castle competitions and old fashioned tans. Youth friendly Beaches are available as well.

Rain Forest

Tanti's Rain Forests are not overly large but remarkably well preserved and available to public tours. Beautiful Waterfalls await!

Tanti Eruptus

Lava often pours from infamous Mt. Tanti. Tours are available through the high ground paths given by the nation's Park Rangers.

Transport

Public and private transportation is available to everyone. Buses follow the most popular tourist routes and Taxis are available on call or at stands.

Sites

Use public transport and find your way to Tanti City. The markets are wonderful and nearby Yellow Leaf Bay includes boat tours and Youth friendly beach.

Dining, Life's Feast

Find it all here

Sea Food

The Ocean has fed Tantians for thousands of years and many of the resident chefs have perfected this process. Enjoy all our native fish and shell fish, fresh and waiting.

Grocery

For a more permanent staple, our fresh local foods are available at grocery stores Island wide. A great deal of wonderful imported foods are availabel as well.

Liquid Refreshments

Our Youth Beaches are all alcohol friendly and have water side pubs and clubs. Tanti also hosts a fantastic micro-brewery.

Imported

Tanti's Resturants also include imported Americana cuisine and on the go foods. Imported but still fresh Pan Asian food is available at several Resturants also.

Frequently Asked Questions

  Power Outlets

Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as in the United States).

  Closing Time

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

  Drinking Age

The drinking age on Taniti is 18, and the drinking age is not strictly enforced.

  Closings

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

  Language

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

  Hospital

There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

  Crime

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

  Currency

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
